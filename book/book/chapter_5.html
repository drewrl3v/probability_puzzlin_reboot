Order Statistics

Problem 1 (Uniform pdf)

Let X1​ be a uniform random variable between 0 and 1, dentoed: X1​∼U[0,1]. What is the probability density function (pdf) of X1​?

Problem 2 (Min and Max)

Let X1​,…,Xn​∼i.i.dU[0,1]. What is the pdf of max(X1​,…,Xn​) and min(X1​,…,Xn​)?

Problem 3 (Multiplication v.s. Square-Root)

Say X1​,X2​∼i.i.dU[0,1]. Then consider the following random variables: Y=max(X1​,X2​) and Z=X2​​. Show that they have the same distribution.

Problem 4 (K-th Order statistic)

Let X1​,…,Xn​∼i.i.dU[0,1]. Now let's order these random variables and label them:

X(1)​,…,X(n)​

Note: X(1)​=min(X1​,…,Xn​) and X(n)​=max(X1​,…,Xn​).

In general what's the cumulative density function (cdf) and the pdf of X(k)​.

Problem 5 (Strange Powers)

Sample 3 numbers uniformly in [0,1], (call them X,Y,Z) and compute W=(XY)Z. What is the distribution of the random variable W?
